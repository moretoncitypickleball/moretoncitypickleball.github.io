About Moreton City Pickleball

Our Mission.

At Moreton City Pickleball, we’re more than just a club—we’re a vibrant community driven by friendship and a shared love for the game. Whether you're a seasoned player or holding a paddle for the first time, you'll find a place on our court and encouragement from the sidelines. Our mission is simple: to grow the sport of pickleball in a fun and supportive environment.

Perfect for all ages, Pickleball is one of the fastest-growing sports in the world—a dynamic blend of tennis, badminton, and ping pong played on a smaller court. It's easy to learn, endlessly social, and addictive in all the right ways.
